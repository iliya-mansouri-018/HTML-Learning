Mehrad Hidden

Mehrad Hidden is a multi-instrumentalist, singer, songwriter and producer of many bands.
Originally he was the co-founder and the lead singer of the Persian hiphop band ZEDBAZI.
Years later he founded a second band called BOZORG alongside Saman Wilson his childhood friend.
Mehrad Hidden is working solo as of 2018.
His solo albums include: Toonel vol.1, Sefr and Toonel Vol.2.
In 2022, he collaborated with the guitarist Moody Moussavi on the album Zoozanaghe.
